# Privacy Policy — Metronome AI

**Last updated:** 2026-06-05

## Overview

Metronome AI is a metronome and reference tone app for musicians. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Voice and text commands
When you use the microphone or type a command, the text of your command (e.g. "set BPM to 120", "give me a C") is sent to a processing server to interpret your intent. This text is used solely to determine what action to perform in the app.

**We do not store, log, or retain your commands.** The text is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

### No other data
We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information.

## Third-Party Services

### Anthropic (AI processing)
Voice commands are processed using [Anthropic's Claude AI](https://www.anthropic.com). Text is transmitted to Anthropic's API to interpret the command and return a structured action. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

Commands are not used to train AI models.

### Cloudflare (network proxy)
Requests pass through a Cloudflare Worker acting as a proxy. Cloudflare may log standard network metadata (IP address, timestamp) per their infrastructure policies. Cloudflare's privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

## Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access solely to capture your spoken commands. Audio is converted to text on-device by Apple's speech recognition before being sent anywhere. Raw audio is never transmitted.

## Data Retention

We retain no user data. Commands are processed transiently and not stored on any server.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository with a new date.
